How To Play

1. Move the airplane using the arrows keys;

2. The timer present the time lapsed;

3. The fuel counter show to remain fuel;

4. During the flight, the aircraft need to avoid the birds that are presented in the sky. If the airplane hit a bird, the game is over;

5. You can shoot the birds by press space key;

6. During the flight, the aircraft need to collect fuel and stars in the sky;

7. You can pause the game clicking in a button pause, or pressing the space bar.

8. Go beyond all limits;
